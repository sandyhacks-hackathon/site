## FAQ

**So—what is a hackathon?** A social coding marathon! Teams of around 4 students will work together to build awesome apps, games, and websites.

**Do I need to know how to code?** Nope! Any student, whether they've been programming for years or have never done it at all, can attend.
**Do I need to pay?** Nope! Thanks to our sponsors, it is 100% free.
