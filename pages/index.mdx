## FAQ

<FAQ>

**So—what is a hackathon?** A social coding marathon! Teams of around two to four students will work together to build awesome apps, games, and websites.

**Do I need to know how to code?** Nope! Any student, whether they've been programming for years or have never done it at all, can attend. You could attend a workshop to learn how to build something with code!

**Do I need to bring a team?** No—most people won't. People with cool ideas will form teams at the start of the event!

**What do I need to bring?** Just a laptop and a charger.

**Do I need to pay?** Nope! Thanks to our sponsors, it is 100% free.

**How many students will be there?** Around 25.

**What about hardware? Will you have Arduinos at the event?** We will! (and if you make an awesome project with it, you can keep it!)

**My question isn't answered here!** Email us at [team@sandyhacks.org](mailto:team@sandyhacks.org)!

</FAQ>
